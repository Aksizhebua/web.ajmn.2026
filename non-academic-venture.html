BALLROOM Y15

The ballroom on the 15th floor of the Yustinus Building is designed to host large-events such as seminars, conferences, product launches, as well as weddings and other formal academic and non-academic occasions.

VENUE SPECIFICATIONS & AMENITIES

Capacity & Dimensions

  • Standing Configuration: Up to 600 guests
  • Theater/Seminar Setup: Up to 750 guests
  • Banquet Round Tables: Up to 250 guests
  • Total Area: 902 m²
    • Main Ballroom: 902 m²
    • Foyer Area: 300 m²

Technical Equipment & Services

  • Audio System: Premium Bose Sound System
  • Visual Equipment: 2 HD Projectors with Screens
  • Reception Area: Dedicated Welcome Desk
  • Climate Control: Central Air Conditioning
  • Display Screens: Smart TVs (VIP Room & Lobby)

J. LO SIANG HIEN GINTING SEMINAR ROOM

The J. Lo Siang Hien Ginting Seminar Room is a multifunctional space with three flexible areas that can be used separately or combined into one. It is suitable for trainings, workshops, discussions, as well as other formal events.

VENUE FACILITIES & FEATURES

Capacity & Dimensions

  • Standing Configuration: Up to 250 guests
  • Theater/Seminar Setup: Up to 150 guests
  • Banquet Round Tables: Up to 96 guests
  • Total Area: 327 m² (main room)
  • Foyer Area: 245 m² lobby space

Technical Equipment & Amenities

  • Climate Control: Central Air Conditioning
  • Furniture: Complete tables & chairs setup
  • Visual Equipment: Projector with screen
  • Audio System: Sound system & microphones
  • Writing Tools: Glassboard & flipchart
  • Prayer Facility: Musholla available

AMPHITHEATER

The amphitheater can be used for various activities such as art performances, small concerts, public lectures, outdoor seminars, and community gatherings. Located on the Unika Atma Jaya campus with an open atmosphere, it provides a relaxed yet organized environment.

VENUE FACILITIES & FEATURES

Seating & Performance Area

  • Seating Capacity: Approximately 240 guests (bench seating)
  • Performance Platform: Dedicated Stage Area
  • Technical Room: Sound & AV Control Room
  • Special Feature: Fire Ring (available upon request)

Accessibility & Safety

  • Accessible Seating: Designated wheelchair area
  • Access Points: Multiple entries (ramps, stairs, wheelchair access)
  • Barrier-Free Path: Accessible ramp throughout venue

Infrastructure & Amenities

  • Perimeter Safety: Metal handrails & retaining walls
  • Privacy Screen: Wood fence screening
